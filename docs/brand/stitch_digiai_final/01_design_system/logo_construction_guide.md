# DIGIAI Logo Construction & System

## 1. The Symbol: Convergence Grid
The DIGIAI symbol is built on an 8x8 grid. It consists of a boundary square containing a central hub and four peripheral nodes.
- **Convergência:** The peripheral nodes represent the various product verticals (Clearix, Lumina, etc.) connecting to the central infrastructure hub.
- **Optical Precision:** The layout subtly references an optical lens reticle or target, signifying focus and geometric accuracy.
- **Quiet Tech:** No gradients or complex shapes. Pure black and white (or brand navy/white) with 0px corner radius.

## 2. Clear Space & Sizing
- **Clear Space:** Defined by 'X', where X is the width of one grid node (1/8th of the symbol width). Clear space of 2X is required around all logo variations.
- **Minimum Sizing:** 
  - **Print:** 10mm height.
  - **Digital:** 24px height for wordmark; 16px for icon-only (monogram).

## 3. Typography
- **Wordmark:** Source Serif 4 (Bold). Tracking set to 0.05em to ensure a stable, editorial feel.
- **System/Data:** JetBrains Mono for technical labeling and versioning.
- **Body:** Inter for UI and functional text.

## 4. Grid System
The symbol is constructed with a stroke weight of 2 units (on an 8-unit internal grid) for the boundary, with solid fills for the internal nodes to provide visual weight and legibility at small scales.
